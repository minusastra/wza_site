Our Purpose

Mission

To transform women's sports in Africa by building engaged communities around female athletes, creating commercial opportunities, and developing sustainable fan ecosystems.

Vision

A continent where every girl can see herself represented in sports, where women athletes are celebrated as cultural icons, and where fandom drives economic empowerment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work and how you can get involved.

How can I support Wecheza's mission?

There are many ways to support our work! You can volunteer at our events, become a sponsor, donate equipment or funds, or simply attend our tournaments and spread the word about women's sports in East Africa.

Do you work with individual athletes or only teams?

We work with both individual athletes and teams. Our mentorship programs focus on individual development, while our equipment donations and tournament organization benefit entire teams and clubs.

How can my organization become a partner?

We're always looking for new partnerships! Please contact us through our form below or email us directly at info@wecheza.com to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

Where are your programs currently active?

We currently operate in Kenya, with a focus on Nairobi and Eldoret. We have plans to expand to Uganda and Tanzania in the coming year as our network and resources grow.

How can I stay updated on your events?

The best way to stay informed is to subscribe to our newsletter below, follow us on social media, or check our website regularly for announcements about upcoming tournaments and initiatives.
